<h3>French Childhood</h3>

* Wren was born in Paris, France as the only child of Antoine and Celine Dubois.
* Her parents were very devoted to her. Celine stayed home with her while Antoine worked.
* Wren lost both her parents when she was seven years old. They were fatally stabbed during a mugging.

<h3>New York City</h3>

* Wren’s only living relative was her paternal uncle, Louis Dubois, who was granted custody of her after her parents’ death.
* He worked as a lawyer in New York City. Wren came to live with him shortly after her parents’ funeral.
* While Louis never neglected Wren’s physical needs, he was not an openly paternal person and could come off cold and distant. He wanted to fulfill the family obligation to take care of Wren, but did not develop a close relationship with her.
* Instead, he hired a nanny for her until she was old enough to care for herself and enrolled her in an online schooling program that focused in English and French culture rather than an American school.
* Wren was very immersed in her studies, though lacking in social interaction with peers her own age due to being homeschooled.

<h3>Teenage Years: </h3>

* Wren met Dominic Abrams when she was 16 years old.
* He was 22 and worked as an intern for Louis’ law firm while in law school.
* He came from a prominent political family that Louis wanted to remain connected with, which caused her uncle to overlook his attentions in Wren.
* Dominic would often find a reason to work closely with Louis, even at his home office, and would talk to Wren whenever he could.
* While he was careful to not ‘cross any lines’ on Wren while she was underage, he made his interests known when it came to her. He would bring her books or small gifts he thought she would enjoy or shower her with attention.
* Having lacked much attention from Louis, Wren quickly considered him her closest friend and later, her lover.
* Dominic asked her out shortly after she turned 18. He was 24 at the time.

<h3>Relationship with Dominic:</h3>

* Dominic proposed to Wren not long after they began dating, right before she was planning to attend college.
* While she had been accepted to art school in Paris, she deferred attending “for a year” after getting engaged.
* Despite the age difference, Louis strongly encouraged their relationship in order to appease Dominic and his family connections.
* Wren married Dominic when she was 19 years old.

<h3>Marriage:</h3>

* Dominic had been attentive and overly sweet to her in their relationship and her marriage was one she eagerly entered.
* Not long after their marriage, Dominic shifted to be more controlling towards Wren.
* Dominic did not let Wren go to college, Paris or otherwise, and pushed her towards staying at home as a socialite as he started to shift into his political career.
* Over time, the relationship turned physically abusive when Dominic didn’t get what he wanted.

<h3>Friendship with Reagan Mitchell</h3>

* Wren met Reagan two years into her marriage when she was 21 years old at a campaign event for Dominic’s brother.
* Reagan came from another prominent family and Dominic wanted to impress her family for his own career.
* Reagan asked to meet Wren for coffee, which Dominic encouraged to gain connection to the family.
* Reagan had witnessed a moment where Dominic roughly grabbed Wren in a hallway earlier in the evening.
* During their coffee meeting, Reagan expressed concern for Wren, which she initially denied. Reagan didn’t push the issue at the time and instead built a friendship with her over time.
* During this time, Wren also developed a relationship with Reagan’s family and friends, especially her girlfriend, Amelia Aldersley.  
* Wren became close with the family, especially Harper and her daughter, Alice.

<h3>Divorce: </h3>

* Nearly a year into their friendship, Wren opened up to Reagan about the abuse and her life with Dominic.
* Reagan discretely connected Wren with a lawyer who could help her separate from Dominic.
* Dominic had made Wren sign a prenup before their marriage, and Louis was no help to her, as he didn’t want to blow up the relationship he had with Dominic’s family.
* Wren nearly backed out of the divorce, but Reagan promised that her family, as well as the Aldersley family, would help her. 
* While they would have helped her for nothing, Wren insisted on finding a way to pay them back and became Alice's nanny in return.

<h3>Art School/Life Now: </h3>

* Wren’s divorce was final almost a year ago. Dominic did everything he could to control the situation and Wren received nothing in the divorce, but her freedom from him was more than worth it to her.
* Reagan and Harper encouraged Wren to apply to NYU during the divorce.
* Wren currently attends NYU, where she is studying art in her second year at the program.
* Wren still lives with Harper and still works as Alice's nanny.
