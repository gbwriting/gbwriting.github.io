<h3>History Information: </h3>
* Diego died when Mena was 16 and found out before she could even tell him about landing a roll for a DCOM - The Heirs
* Switched to homeschooling once she got her first role and graduated early and did everything she could to make her family proud
* On set she put on a happy face to hide how truly hurt she was by losing her brother, secretly started doing drugs and eventually started to party
* After finishing the first Heirs film, Mena was just about to turn 18 and went out to party and celebrate becoming a legal adult. Already drunk and high, someone slipped something into her drink. She doesn't remember much of that night, but she remembers waking up in a hospital. When she woke up, her mother was asleep in a chair beside the bed and Nova, was asleep with her head on the hospital bed and holding her hand. Everything she was told later changed Mena for the rest of her life.
