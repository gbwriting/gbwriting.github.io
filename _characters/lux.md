Lux Violet Carter was born in the early morning of August 24th, 2000 in London, a result of a one night stand during her mother Audrey’s freshman year of college. Lux never knew her father, and her mom has always claimed she doesn’t even remember his name. Whether or not that’s true has never really mattered. Audrey was all Lux ever needed. Despite it being just the two of them, it never really felt that way. The restaurant where Audrey worked became a second home, and its owners and staff treated Lux and Audrey like family.

From a young age, Audrey nurtured Lux’s creativity in any form it took - whether that was painting (even on the walls of their apartment) or sculpting pottery with mud in the backyard. Lux was always making something or trying out a new medium, but it was music that really stuck. When Audrey dragged home a beat-up acoustic guitar from a yard sale for Lux’s eighth birthday, it became clear that music was what Lux was always meant to do.

Growing up as the daughter of a single mother, money could definitely be tight, but Lux never felt as if she was limited in her future choices. Audrey worked to save up what she could to send her daughter to college and encouraged her daughter to apply to the best schools. When Lux got into college in New York City, she was reluctant to move across the literal world from her mom. Audrey wouldn’t let her turn it down, especially with the impressive scholarship they’d given her, and Lux still notes that to be one of their first and only fights.

Despite her homesickness, Lux loved New York City. The music scene became their second home, she quickly proved herself as a promising audio engineer. It was through parties and the music scene that she met Camie Taylor, who quickly became her best friend and later, her "unofficial girlfriend". 

Though Lux loves to produce music and works as the head audio engineer at a local music venue, her heart remains in writing and performing. Lux’s passion is music, and while they want to be a performer, they’ll take anything they can get to make their dreams happen in the music industry.
