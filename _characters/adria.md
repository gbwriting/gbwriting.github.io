<h3>Childhood:</h3>

* Andrea's child from her second marriage as well as the youngest
* Grew up with an unending obsession with water and the ocean
* Very close-knit family that would do anything for each other
* On a trip to her grandparents house there was a bad car accident
* Michelle and Charlie weren't far behind them and Michelle ended up being the one to pull Adria out of the car
* Michelle kept Adria, the only survivor, alive until paramedics were able to rush her to the hospital
* Since Adria was allergic to the majority of human medications (a rare trait that can be found in witches), magic had to be used alongside it to keep her alive
* When she finally woke up in the hospital, Charlie was sitting beside her bed - it was then that Adria decided that; "You don't look like a Charlotte. I'm going to call you Charlie."
* They quickly became inseparable and since Adria was now an orphan - the Mackenzie family decided to adopt her

<h3>Teenage Years:</h3>

* As she got older, Adria became glaringly aware of the abuse Christopher bestows on Michelle and even Charlie
* She's often treated as fragile, breakable, because of her magically damaged heart
* Throughout the years there are many hospital visits because of her heart - it becomes obvious that it's a ticking time bomb
* One night, Charlie and Adria decide to do a ritual to bind Charlie to Adria as her familiar in an attempt to help give her strength - what they didn't realize is they ended up doing a spell/ritual that was much more powerful
* When the abuse gets to a boiling point, Michelle finally files for divorce before moving to Boston, MA where her twin brother Devin lived with his adopted daughter Tatum
* Boston is where Charlie and Adria finish out high school and where the sisters were accepted to Cornell University

<h3>Now:</h3>

* Adria still struggles with her heart, forever a time bomb ticking over her head - though she lives her life to the fullest
* After graduating with her MS she got a job at New York Aquarium as a Marine Educator & Public Programs Specialist while also working towards her doctorate
* Constantly monitors her heart, but does it in a subtle way - she doesn't want to worry her family
