Eilian's history is a bit complicated, but with who she really is, how could it not be?

Most people know her as Eliana Aldersley, the CEO of the Aldersley-Brandon Foundation, who has spent decades of her life advocating and campaigning to make the world safer for supernaturals like herself. 

But what they don’t know is that she’s far older than they think, and far more powerful. 

Eilian can’t tell you the dates of most of her life, but she can tell you that she was born (or at least came to be) in Northern Ireland and was taken in by the McNeal family and their two daughters. They raised the girl, who they thought was an orphan, as if she was their own daughter, all while being aware of the fact that she wasn’t truly human at all.

Eilian had no idea what she was, or what she could do, until she created the Fae species — with her sisters being the first of their kind. She turned them first, then the other children of her village, and created the first Fae court, of which she was the queen. 

As a young woman, Eilian fell in love with Jo Carter (later Darcey) and the two were rarely apart. 

In the centuries that followed, Eilian did her best to protect the Fae, which grew and grew as those she turned had children of their own. Fae children were exceptionally powerful and as a result, they were hunted for their blood and their magic. 

In the 1800s, Eilian met a hunter named Jackson Aldridge. Unlike most hunters, Jackson believed that supernatural creatures were good and wanted to keep them safe. Eventually, guided by the universe itself, Eilian agreed to marry Jackson. Their marriage was for protection and convenience — protecting not just Eilian as a Fae woman, but also both of them in their attraction to the same sex. While their marriage wasn’t intimate, it did result in Jo spending some time apart from Eilian in England, though Jo did eventually move to New York City. 

Eilian, with the help of Jo, her sisters, and Jackson, advocated for the safety and rights of supernaturals. Over time, the Aldersley-Brandon Foundation was created as a nonprofit to aid in these endeavors. Slowly, supernatural creatures were safer to come out of hiding and exist as they truly are — even if there’s still a long road to go. 

Feeling as if things were safer, Eilian and Jo decided to have children with Jackson’s help. They were no longer legally married, though still remained very close friends. Their first daughter, Amelia, was born in October 1998. 

During her pregnancy with her second daughter, Piper, Jo began to experience visions of a prophesy that existed with Amelia and Piper. Amelia was already showing signs of being exceptionally powerful, and the visions showed a catastrophic future if the girls grew up together. While they did everything in their power to avoid it, it was determined that Jo would take Piper to England and raise her there while they tried to figure out a way to keep both girls safe. 

In the 25 years that have followed, Eilian hasn’t seen her youngest daughter — except for in photos and videos. Jo, additionally, hasn’t seen Amelia. Unable to remain truly apart, Eilian and Jo spend as much time as they can together while remaining a safe distance from their daughters, but it’s not enough for Eilian. 

She has no idea how to bring her family back together, but she’ll do anything in her power to make it happen.
