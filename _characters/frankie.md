<h3>History Information: </h3>
* Lost her parents in a murder/suicide which put her and Dylan into the foster system
* Were taken in by the Camden family, Alex was Dylan's best friend as well as boyfriend
* Frankie was determined to show she can take care of her brother, and made an agreement with the Camden family that they would support her emancipation if she showed she could support herself - this was done lovingly
* To do this, she did everything she could and worked her ass off
* While working, she met a woman named Mariah Dulay - they started a relationship of sorts, Mariah was Frankie's domme and in return for sexual favors, Mariah made sure that Frankie had an apartment and enough money to pay bills
* All this resulted in her finally being emancipated at 17
* Frankie also got a job at the record store which was owned by Alex's parents
* Eventually, the relationship with Mariah ended after 3 years due to Mariah moving - by then Frankie had a decent savings
* Frankie and Nyx met when Nyx got a job at the record store, they hit it off, and it didn't take long for them to learn about the others' passion for music - then Echoed Skies was formed
