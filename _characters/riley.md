<h3>History Information: </h3>
* In something of a Domme/Sub relationship within her relationship with Waverly, she is the submissive
* Struggles with addition and is very good at hiding it from people
* Riley has a complicated relationship with her modeling career. Riley loves fashion, editorial shoots, creating art, working with photographers, traveling, meeting creatives, the collaborative aspect, and the ability to become someone else for a day. What she hates is being treated like a product because eventually she stops feeling like Riley - she feels like a mannequin.
* When Riley was 19 she was already modeling professionally - she's exhausted, jet-lagged, working sixteen-hour days, flying from one country to another. Then someone offers her cocaine and says, "This'll wake you up." She says no. The second time - still no. The fifth time - maybe. Nothing terrible happens. She feels...amazing. Confident. Funny. Focused. Energetic. She finishes the shoot. Everyone laughs. She thinks... "People overreact." Months later... she's buying it herself.
* Riley hides her addiction from Waverly. If she knew she'd help immediately - and Riley doesn't want help. She wants one more day. One more shoot. One more week. Then she'll stop. Except she never quite gets there.
