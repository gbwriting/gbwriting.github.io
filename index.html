greenborough

a collection of characters, places, and information about greenborough's world.

Greenborough exists as a world where supernaturals have created lives among humans.

Greenborough was created in April 2022 by Kaci & Sarah Loveless-Hall. This site is an archive of character information, short stories, lore, and other information regarding this writing world.
