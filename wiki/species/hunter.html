Reproduction & Lineage

More Text Here

  • Humans: Pairings between a human and a Hunter invariably result in a standard human child.
  • Weres: Genetic traits for Weres are completely dominant. A child born to a Hunter and a Were will always inherit full Were status, with no known exceptions.
  • Witches: Genetic traits for Witches are also completely dominant. A child born to a Hunter and a Witch will always be a Witch themselves.
  • Fae: Pairings with the Fae are highly unpredictable. In the vast majority of cases, the child will be born entirely human. A Fae child is only possible if both the human and Fae parent carry latent lineages directly tied to the original, ancient Fae bloodlines—an occurrence that remains exceptionally rare.
