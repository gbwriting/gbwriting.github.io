More Text Here

  • Humans: Pairings with the Fae are highly unpredictable. In the vast majority of cases, the child will be born entirely human. A Fae child is only possible if both the human and Fae parent carry latent lineages directly tied to the original, ancient Fae bloodlines—an occurrence that remains exceptionally rare.
  • Weres: Genetic traits for Weres are completely dominant. A child born to a Fae and a Were will always inherit full Were status, with no known exceptions.
  • Witches: Genetic traits for Witches are also completely dominant. A child born to a Fae and a Witch will always be a Witch themselves.
