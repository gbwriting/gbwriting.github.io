Humans are fully mortal beings characterized by a complete lack of supernatural traits or abilities. Making up the vast majority of the world’s population, they outnumber every supernatural species by an overwhelming margin...

History

Though origin stories vary across cultures, humans have inhabited Earth for thousands of years...

Abilities, Strengths, & Weaknesses

Humans possess no innate supernatural traits or magical capabilities...

Strengths

  • Anomalous Safety: Lacking a magical aura makes them less visible to threats.

Weaknesses

  • Physical Fragility: Human bodies are highly susceptible to standard illnesses.

Humans have average lifespans, typically living between 80 to 90 years...
